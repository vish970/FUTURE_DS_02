#  Customer Retention & Churn Analysis

##  Overview
This project analyzes customer churn behavior in a subscription-based business using the Telco Customer Churn dataset.

The objective is to identify churn patterns, understand customer retention, and provide actionable insights to reduce customer loss.

---

##  Objectives
- Analyze customer churn rate
- Identify high-risk customer segments
- Understand retention patterns
- Provide business recommendations

---

##  Dataset
- Telco Customer Churn Dataset (Kaggle)
- Includes customer demographics, subscription details, and churn status

---

##  Tools Used
- Python
- Pandas
- NumPy
- Matplotlib
- Seaborn

---

##  Key Metrics
- **Churn Rate:** 26.58%
- **Total Customers Analyzed:** 7032

##  Key Insights
- High churn occurs in early customer lifecycle
- Month-to-month contracts have the highest churn
- Customers with higher charges are more likely to leave
- Long-term customers show strong retention behavior

---

##  Recommendations
- Improve onboarding experience for new customers
- Offer incentives for long-term contracts
- Target high-risk customers with personalized offers
- Introduce loyalty programs for retention
- Optimize pricing strategies
